APPENDECTOMY

Appendectomy involves the open or laparoscopic removal of the appendix. This operation is performed to remove an infected or inflamed appendix, commonly referred to as appendicitis.

CHOLECYSTECTOMY

Cholecystectomy involves the open or laparoscopic removal of the gallbladder. This operation is performed due to the presence of gallstones and the health effects associated.

CORONARY ARTERY BYPASS

Coronary Artery Bypass surgery is performed to redirect blood flow from a blocked or obstructed artery. This operation is performed to improve blood flow directed to the heart.
